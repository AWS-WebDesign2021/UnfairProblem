Regions

The hyacinth macaw is mostly located in Central and South America. The biggest regions where these parrots can be found are in:

  1. Brazil
  2. Bolivia
  3. Paraguay

Since these macaws are completely dependent on fruits, seeds, and nuts, their main habitat is the South American tropical rainforest, where many wildlife resides.
