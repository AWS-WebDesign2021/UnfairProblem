Collector's Guide: South American Masa and it's uses in Culinary Food

Sofia La Rosa



Arepa

Arepas are unique to South American food and culture. They're made out of masa,
a dough made from corn, and are eaten year round. This food has many different
versions and all are equally delicious.

Hallaca

Hallacas are a typical Venezuelan food. They're stuffed with protein, capers, raisins, olives, and are surrounded
by a leaf when cooked.
