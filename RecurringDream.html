A Pyterodactyl's Final Moments

By Sofia La Rosa

Soaring above the trees, a giant orange pyterodactyl looks down on the land. Although he had been hunted by other, greater, dinosaurs, he considered this his home. Life was peaceful flying above the trees, watching the sunrise and set each day as the wind gently grazed its wings. The sunset painted the sky with oranges and yellows and were soon to be followed with dark blues, and stars and moons that would light up the sky. As the lights began to appear, he charted his course for home; Smiling at another day of life. Little did he know, this peaceful day would be interupted by chaos and terror, that would be known as his final moments of life.
